About Cátedra RTVE-UC3M

This collaboration is a joint effort between RTVE and
the Knowledge Reuse Reseach Group
(Carlos III University of Madrid)
to boost research in the field of Big Data, Linked Data, Complex network analysis, Natural Language Processing, etc. applied to social network analysis.
